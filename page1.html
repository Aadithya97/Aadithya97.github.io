About Me

Here's a bit more about me!

I am Aadithya Kandeth, a highly skilled and dedicated Masters in Computer Science student at the University of Florida, with a strong foundation in Information Technology from SRM Institute of Science and Technology. My academic and professional journey is marked by a series of accomplishments and diverse experiences in the field of software development and data science.

Educational Excellence: Achieved a notable GPA of 3.7/4 in my Masters and 87.62/100 in my Bachelors, coupled with an Academic Achievement Scholarship at UF for maintaining academic distinction.
Robust Technical Skillset: Proficient in a wide range of programming languages and tools including C#, .NET, Python, Java, Angular, Machine Learning, and Big Data technologies. My experience spans across various aspects of software engineering such as Agile development, Data Engineering, and Linux systems.
Professional Expertise: My tenure at Siemens Healthineers as a Software Engineer in R&D for Molecular Imaging and Ultrasound, along with internships at Wipro Limited and The Last Mile Consultants, has honed my skills in software development, data visualization, and big data.
Developed software for PET and SPECT medical scanners, leading API and backend development for over 14 functional components. Significantly reduced product defects and QA time through effective coding and automated testing.
Innovative Project Work: My project portfolio demonstrates my ability to innovate and solve real-world problems. This includes building a twitter clone on a distributed system, developing a social networking site, and creating an intelligent traffic management system using IoT and image processing.
Research Contributions: Authored a paper on dynamic traffic light adjustment using CCTV cameras, showcasing my research capabilities and commitment to practical solutions in traffic management.
Recognition and Awards: My work ethic and performance have been recognized with multiple SPOT awards and a DC excellence award at Siemens Healthineers.

I am passionate about leveraging technology to create impactful solutions, continuously seeking to grow my expertise and contribute to the field of computer science. My journey reflects a blend of academic rigor, professional excellence, and a commitment to innovation and continuous learning.
